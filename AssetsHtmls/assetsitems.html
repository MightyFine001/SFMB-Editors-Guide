Coin - floats wherever it's placed. When collected, adds 1 to the coin counter.
10-50 Coin - larger special coins that add a higher amount of coins. Available in 10, 30, and 50 values.
Mushroom - makes Mario two blocks tall, and grants an extra hit point. You can break bricks by jumping or spin-jumping. The mushroom is also the default for power-ups that have a “or Mushroom” variant. “or Mushroom” variants of power-ups will grant you a mushroom, instead of the desired power-up if you are small.
Fire Flower - lets Mario shoot fireballs. It also melts Ice Crates.
Super Star - grants Mario temporary invincibility, allowing him to defeat enemies on contact. However, you will still die to instant death hazards.
Heart - collecting one adds a heart to your counter, unless you’re already full (only functional with "Use Health Point Mode" ability flag).
Heart Container - collecting it adds an extra heart slot
1-UP - grants an extra life.
3-UP - grants three extra lives.
Pink Coin - collecting all of them in a level gives the player a key to open locked doors.
Key - when collected, use to open locked doors.
Super Leaf - gives Mario a tail and lets him fly after gaining enough speed, though not forever. Press jump midair to hover briefly, and use the tail to whack enemies!
Frog Suit - boosts water movement and jump height, but decreases land movement speed.
Hammer Suit - lets Mario throw hammers in an arch. Can defeat enemies that are usually immune to power-up related attacks.
Tanooki Suit - gives Mario a tail, and lets him fly temporarily after gaining enough speed. Press jump to hover briefly, and use the tail to whack enemies! Additionally, it grants the ability to turn into a statue. While in statue form, you`re temporarily invulnurable to non instant kill damage sources.
Penguin Suit - gives Mario faster and more precise swimming. Furthermore, grants the ability to throw ice balls to freeze enemies, and a belly slide that defeats foes and break blocks.
Propeller Mushroom - lets Mario soar upward. After rising, you can float down slowly or dive to break bricks.
Ice Flower - lets you shoot ice balls to encase enemies in ice. You can pick up the block and throw it, or use it as a platform.
Cloud Flower - lets Mario create up to three cloud platforms. The platforms are temporary, and are available for reuse after they expire.
Boomerang Flower - lets you throw boomerangs. They can pass through blocks, but only one can be thrown at a time.
Wierd Mushroom - makes Mario tall, lanky, and boosts jump height.
Bubble Flower - lets Mario blow bubbles that can trap enemies or items. You can jump on bubbles for extra height, but these bubbles are temporary, causing the contents to vanish.
Mystery Mushroom - This mushroom lets Mario wear one out of over 150 costumes. It`s purely visual, and grants no special abilities.
Mega Mushroom - makes Mario temporarily huge. He can smash through blocks, enemies, pipes, and more. After the timer runs out, he returns to normal size.
POW Block - when you pick it up and throw it, it damages all on-screen enemies and causes coins to fall.
Red POW Block - a floating variant of the POW Block that can`t be picked up. Hitting it creates a shockwave, triggering nearby Red POW Blocks within 3 blocks.
Yellow POW Block - a floating variant you can`t pick up. It can be hit multiple times before disappearing.
P-Switch - pressing it plays a short jingle and toggles all coins to bricks and vice versa, also switching P-Blocks` states. It ends when the jingle does.
P-Block - a block that toggles between solid and non-solid when a P-Switch is activated.
Spring - a bouncy item, that helps reach high platforms. Can be picked up when standing upright, but not when sideways.
Light Block - a glowing block that lights up dark areas. It can be picked up when small, but not when enlarged.
Poison Mushroom - damages you on contact.
Rotten Mushroom - follows the player and deals damage on contact.
