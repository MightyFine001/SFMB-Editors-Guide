Goomba - walks back and forth and falls off cliffs. Defeated with one stomp. When winged, it makes two small hops, then one big hop.
Galoomba - cannot be stomped to defeat. When hit, flips over and can be kicked or grabbed. Falls off cliffs. When winged, it makes two small hops, then one big hop.
Goombrat - walks back and forth but won’t fall off cliffs. Defeated with one stomp. When winged, it makes two medium hops, then one big hop.
Goombud - cannot be stomped to defeat. Flips over when hit and can be kicked or grabbed. Won’t fall off cliffs. When winged, it makes two medium hops, then one big hop.
Koopa - walks back and forth. Jumping on them makes them hide in their shells, which can be kicked or picked up. When the shell is moving, it can kill enemies and break bricks. Shells break bricks and damage enemies, but spin jumps defeat them outright. If left alone, they pop out after about 6 seconds. Winged Koopas perform repeated large hops.
Red Koopa - winged Red Koopas fly either horizontally or vertically. Distance is adjustable.
Dry Bones - crumble when jumped on but revive after a few seconds. Immune to fireballs. Winged versions fly horizontally or vertically. Distance is adjustable. Powered-Up versions do not crumble when jumped on.
Buzzy Beetle - hide in their shells when jumped on. Shells can be kicked or thrown. When the shell is moving, it can kill enemies and break bricks. Winged ones fly left and can safely stood on.
Spiny - covered in spikes that damage on contact if jumped on. Winged versions fly left and sometimes hide in their shells, firing spikes in all directions.
Piranha Plant - move up and down if placed on pipes, otherwise stay put. Can be spin-jumped on without taking damage. Winged versions do occasional big hops.
Aggressive Piranha Plant - move in and out of pipes faster and more often. Winged versions do quick, big hops.
Fire Piranha Plant - shoot fireballs when emerging from pipes or the ground. Winged versions hop, hover briefly, then fire.
Muncher - act like solid blocks but can be moved. On pipes, they emerge briefly before retreating. Winged versions hop, hover, then descend.
Jumping Piranha Plants - can jump on their own and descend slowly. Winged versions hover briefly before descending.
Hammer Bro - throw hammers and occasionally jump to nearby platforms. Winged versions jump nearly twice as high.
Boomerang Bro - throw boomerangs and occasionally jump to nearby platforms. Winged versions jump nearly twice as high.
Fire Bro - spit fireballs and occasionally jump to nearby platforms. Winged versions jump nearly twice as high.
Ice Bro - throw ice balls and occasionally jump to nearby platforms. Winged versions jump nearly twice as high.
Sledge Bro - throws hammers and occasionally jumps and slams down, stunning the player. Winged versions jump lower but still stun.
Heavy Boomerang Bro - throw boomerangs and occasionally jump and slam down, stunning the player. Winged versions jump lower but still stun.
Heavy Fire Bro - spit fireballs and occasionally jump and slam down, stunning the player. Winged versions jump lower but still stun.
Heavy Ice Bro - throw ice balls and occasionally jump and slam down, stunning the player. Winged versions jump lower but still stun.
Lakitu - drop Spiny eggs or other items. Winged versions move in a wavy flight pattern.
Podoboo - leap from lava to a set height before diving back in. Winged versions bounce diagonally around the stage.
Thwomp - drop when the player passes underneath, crushing bricks on impact. Can also move upward or sideways. Winged versions drift in a wavy pattern. Powered-Up versions smash more blocks and crush multiple layers.
Cheep Cheep - swim in one direction underwater. In levels with water at the bottom, they hop twice with small hops, then once with a big. Winged versions leap higher and stay airborne longer.
Red Cheep Cheep - swim back and forth underwater, tracking the player loosely. In levels with water at the bottom, they hop twice with small hops, then once with a big. Winged versions leap higher and stay airborne longer.
Fish Bone - patrol back and forth. Dash toward the player when in sight, then return to normal if they miss. Winged versions swim and dash faster.
Blooper - chase the player underwater and through the air. Winged versions move farther, higher, and faster.
Bob-Omb - ignite and explode after 4 seconds when jumped on, blowing up bricks and enemies nearby. Can be set to ignite automatically. Winged versions fly. Jumping on them removes wings but doesn’t ignite unless hit again.
Chain Chomp - charge toward the player when close, limited by a chain. Ground-pounding the post frees them. Winged versions float around their post, or fly in a wavy pattern if unchained.
Boo - chase the player when not being looked at. Stop and cover when looked at. Circling Buddies variant rotates around a central point with a gap.
Stretch - occasionally emerge to slide across surfaces before retreating.
Eerie - move in straight lines or waves. Winged versions move faster with larger waves.
Rocky Wrench - pop up to throw a wrench, then retreat. Can only be jumped on when above ground.
Monty Mole - chase the player quickly, bouncing off walls. Winged versions run slower but hop while chasing.
Angry Sun - ???
Wiggler - stomping makes them angry and faster. Bouncing on them gives extra height. Winged versions hop in large leaps.
Magikoopa - transform blocks or enemies into other objects or enemies. Winged versions give wings to their magic and any spawned enemies.
Chargin’ Chuck - charges at the player. Variants include:
  • Diggin’ Chuck: digs up boulders.
  • Passin’ Chuck: kicks rugby balls.
  • Splittin’ Chuck: splits into three.
  • Bouncin’ Chuck: leaps at the player.
  • Clappin’ Chuck: mimics jumps.
  • Confused Chuck: throws baseballs rapidly.
Wings don’t affect Chucks.
Pokey - range from one to 25 segments tall. Can’t be jumped on. Winged versions hop.
Rex - walk until stomped, then run faster. Two stomps to defeat. Winged versions hop and flutter.
Boom Boom - a mini-boss who attacks in cycles, hiding in his shell between moves. Three hits to defeat. Winged versions jump, hover, then drop.
Bowser - attacks by shooting fire, throwing hammers, or both. Defeated by fireballs or lava. Winged versions hop and hover.
Bowser Jr. - attacks by shooting fire, throwing hammers, or both. Moves between attacks. Three hits to defeat. After each hit, he retreats into his shell and moves before reappearing. Can jump and stomp to stun. Winged versions stay airborne longer and hop bigger.
Dry Bowser - attacks by shooting blue fire and throwing bones. Immune to fireballs. Defeated only by lava. Winged versions hover between attacks.
Custom Enemy - also called “CE.” Fully customizable enemy with editable appearance, movement, attacks, health, and other behaviors. By default, it acts like an invisible Goomba. Templates can be built entirely from scratch to create new enemy types. Details on using Custom Enemy templates are explained later in the “Tools” section.
