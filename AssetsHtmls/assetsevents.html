Ground - serves as the main surface players walk on. New levels start with a 3-tile-high layer of it, which you can edit freely. Its appearance changes with the theme and auto-connects.
Brick block - holds items, or be left empty. They can also be destroyed, but only powered-up Mario can break empty bricks.
?-block - by default, it holds a coin, but you can place enemies, and items inside too.
Hard block - breakable block thats look changes with the theme. They can be destroyed by certain enemies, or by wearing a Spiny Shell.
Taken ?-block - appears after hitting an item block, but you can also place it manually.
Invisbile ?-block - invisible until hit. Only the player can trigger it while hidden. Once activated, it turns into a Taken Item Block.
Cloud - considered a semi-solid; meaning you can stand on top of it, but it has no collision on the sides or bottom, so you can jump through it.
Donut block - may look like a donut, but it doesn`t taste like one! Standing on it will cause it to shake, and start falling! After falling, it will respawn.
Flip block - spins when hit, letting you pass through. After a short time, it stops and becomes solid again. Spin jumping on it can destroy it, only if you're powered-up.
Ice block - has a slippery surface, harder to control momentum when on top of.
Spike block - damaged upon contact, but can safely walk over them with a Super Star.
Note block - jumping on Note Blocks bounces you high and plays a musical note!
Music block - plays a unique sound depending on what lands on it, adjusting its y position changes its pitch automatically.
Music block tuner - allows you to manually change the octave of each instrument, removing the need of vertical high level to get the sound of the octave you want!
Death block - touching this block will instantly kill you, even if you have a Super Star!
Safe block - touching this block won`t harm you, but it will kill any enemy that touches it, even bosses!
Marimba block - plays notes as you run across them. When you're ready, jump to launch high into the air!
